27/03/26 Version 1.4

Important Updates regarding latest patch v1.4:

All apps must be copied to PC folder of IGI2 for them to work. Ranker and Map updater app are also available at: https://www.donclanigi2.weebly.com

I.	IGI2 Ranker:

To save your stats online download this app and copy it in your PC folder, follow the link below to download ranker:

https://github.com/Zaidi-45/IGI2-Ranker-Tool/raw/refs/heads/main/IGI2_Ranker.exe

II.	Map Updater:

Download all Multiplayer maps directly from map updater app, so no need to browse the internet to find maps, all are available for download via single app. Copy maps updater to PC folder. Follow link to download Map Updater:

https://www.mediafire.com/file/zmr0aj0iccuy8d7/IGI2MapUpdater.exe/file

III.	Updater:

Install Updater to update your IGI2 Client with latest updates including Maps and game features. Follow the link to download IGI2 Updater and place it in PC folder.

https://drive.google.com/file/d/1BGD8TeMC64zfX_etDhwmktaskOHSBaYj/view?usp=sharing

IV.	DGVoodoo: 

Files of dgvoodoo are included in the Installation, you can use the dgvoodoocpl.exe to set Directory to your game pc folder. Then Configure it, follow the link for detailed guide for setting up dgvoodoo for your Game.

	https://dege.fw.hu/dgVoodoo2/QuickGuide/

	https://dege.fw.hu/dgVoodoo2/ReadmeDirectX/
